MISSION AND VISION

  • To make avail food for everyone, so that one sleeps without feeding their tummy.
  • A vision to provide education to everyone, that no one left uneducated that can lead a major step for nation building. Mission to empower women.
  • Making essential requirements for supporting economically weak families.

NEED OF THE PLATFORM

These days there are many people who want to donate but they are not able to find which NGO to donate. Our main goal is to provide the connection between people and NGOs. On our platform, people just have to login by entering basic details and donate whatever things they have to. There will be complete transparency in our system that whatever the person has donated, it has reached to what NGO. Then the NGOs perform their role and look after how to use the donation which they have received.
